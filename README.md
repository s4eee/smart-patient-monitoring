# AI-Based Smart Patient Monitoring and Early Health Risk Detection System

## Project Overview
This project aims to develop a system for continuous patient monitoring and early health risk detection. The system addresses the challenges patients face after hospital discharge, such as delayed treatment and lack of continuous monitoring, by providing a centralized digital platform.

## Key Features
**Continuous Health Tracking:** Patients can regularly record symptoms, pain levels, body temperature, blood pressure, blood glucose levels, oxygen saturation, and heart rate.
**Post-Operative Monitoring Module:** A specialized module allowing patients to upload wound images, record recovery symptoms, and track healing progress. AI-based analysis identifies warning signs such as infection, swelling, or delayed healing.
**Chronic Disease Support:** Provides medication reminders and monitoring for conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, asthma, and heart disease.
**Doctor Dashboard:** Enables healthcare professionals to view recovery trends, health records, alerts, and AI-generated risk assessments to make informed clinical decisions.

## Methodology: Agile Software Development
This project utilizes the **Agile Software Development Model**. 
This methodology is selected because the system consists of several interconnected modules—including vital sign monitoring, AI-based health analysis, and doctor dashboards—that can be developed incrementally.

Agile allows our team to:
Deliver functional software in small, testable increment.
Continuously incorporate feedback from stakeholders, such as doctors, patients, and mentors.
Detect and resolve defects early in the process.
Adapt to changing healthcare requirements and evolving clinical needs.
Refine AI-based risk prediction models gradually without disrupting other modules.
